**友邦人寿**

**岗位名称：**精算再保岗

**工作地点：**上海

**部门：**Actuarial

**Roles and Responsibility：**

Be responsible for all of the reinsurance related affairs in order to ensure reinsurance to be arranged with the best interest of the Company and the reinsurance accounting to be handled accurately and timely.

1. Ensure the reinsurance arrangement compliance with the Group guideline and regulatory requirements.
2. Negotiates with reinsurers for a new reinsurance needs
3. Ensures the in force treaty for a better term to the Company.
4. Ensures the accuracy of the reinsurance related accrual and timely bill settlements.
5. Completes the reinsurance related reports to Group and regulators.

**Minimum Job Requirements:**

1. University graduate with 1+ years experience, preferably in Actuarial, Mathematics or Statistics.
2. Associate of SOA or equivalent.
3. In depth knowledge of China insurance regulation relevant to actuarial practice and other related regulations.
4. Thorough knowledge in the data processing for statistical analysis.
5. Problem solving and analytical skills, leadership and team work ability.
6. Good presentation and communication skills in Mandarin and English.

**工作性质：**全职

**招聘人数：**1人

5. 产品报告：按时完成监管规定的年度产品报告和其他临时性报告；按时完成公司内部产品相关报告；
